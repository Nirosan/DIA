DIA. Digital Irrigation Automation.

DIA project is started to automate the local irrigation system to protect our water ways and provide a high technology based system to the local people. We deliver the technology to the people to make the gardens automated with Arduino and make an interligent environment by adding high quality sensers to Arduino.

Powered by Arduino. The famous open hardware.

World famous 3D printing and robotic use arduino to as their micro-controller now we give it to your gardens to make it much wonderfull.

Ideamart. Based on Telco Platform.

The whole system is based on Telco platform. All the device is connected with our server. LBS (Location Based System) give the location of your device and check weather to save your water. total system is controlled by SMS.
